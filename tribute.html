Tribute to Maulana Abul Kalam Azad

India's First Education Minister

(1888 - 1958)

About Maulana Abul Kalam Azad

Maulana Abul Kalam Azad was an Indian scholar, freedom fighter, and senior leader of the Indian National Congress. He was the first Minister of Education in the Indian government and played a crucial role in the establishment of a national education system.

Contributions to Education

  • First Minister of Education in independent India.
  • Instrumental in the establishment of the University Grants Commission (UGC).
  • Advocated for free and compulsory primary education.
  • Played a key role in the development of higher education and scientific research in India.

Legacy

Maulana Azad's contributions to education in India are remembered every year on his birth anniversary, November 11, celebrated as National Education Day. His legacy continues to inspire generations of educators and students in India.
